iDestination Cab Application - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 29-may-2023

Welcome to iDestination, a cab application that is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the data you provide while using our application and services. By accessing or using the iDestination cab application, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information: When you register for an iDestination account, we may collect personal information, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.

1.2 Location Data: To provide transportation services, we collect and use your precise location information when you use the iDestination application. You can enable or disable location services through your device settings or the application.

1.3 Usage Data: We may collect information about your interactions with the iDestination application, such as the rides you take, app features you use, and your preferences.

1.4 Device Information: We may collect device-specific information, including your device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

1.5 Log Data: Our servers automatically record certain information when you access or use the iDestination application. This may include your IP address, device information, app features used, and actions taken.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Provide Services: We use the information collected to facilitate the arrangement of rides and provide our transportation services to you.

2.2 Personalization: We may use your information to personalize your experience with the iDestination application, such as suggesting preferred pickup locations or driver preferences.

2.3 Customer Support: Your information helps us respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and improve our customer support services.

2.4 Communication: We may use your contact information to send you transactional emails, updates, and promotional offers related to iDestination services.

2.5 Safety and Security: Your data is used to ensure the safety and security of our users, drivers, and the iDestination platform.

2.6 Analytics: We may analyze data to improve the efficiency and functionality of the iDestination application.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 With Drivers: To arrange rides, your name and location data will be shared with drivers to fulfill your transportation requests.

3.2 Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing the iDestination services, such as payment processing, analytics, and customer support.

3.3 Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

4. Data Retention

4.1 We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. Your Choices

5.1 Account Information: You can review and update your account information within the iDestination application.

5.2 Location Services: You can enable or disable location services through your device settings or the application. However, disabling location services may affect the functionality of the iDestination application.

5.3 Communication: You may choose to opt-out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in our communications.

6. Security

6.1 We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Children's Privacy

7.1 The iDestination application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

8.1 We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated policy will be posted on the iDestination application.
