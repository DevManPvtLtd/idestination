    Here are some FAQ to get started with us.

    We know that renting a car can come with many questions and concerns, which is why we've put together this FAQ section to help you out. If you don't see the answer you're looking for, feel free to reach out to our customer service team for additional assistance.

    • 01 What is the minimum age for renting a car?

      You must be atleast 18 according to the Indian law to rent a vehicle. If you plan to hire a chauffeur then you won't need this criteria.

    • 02 What documents do I need to rent a car?

      You will typically need a valid driver's license, a credit card, and proof of insurance.

    • 03 What happens if I return the car late?

      If you return the car late, you may be charged additional fees or penalties, depending on the rental company's policy. It's important to read the rental agreement carefully to understand the terms and conditions.
