Customer Support Policy for iDestination

At iDestination, we are committed to providing exceptional customer support to ensure a seamless and satisfactory experience for all our users. Our customer support team is available to assist you with any queries, concerns, or issues related to the application, the user app, and the driver app. This policy outlines the channels through which you can access customer support and the hours during which assistance is available.

1. Support Channels:

a. In-App Support: For general inquiries and assistance related to iDestination, users can access our in-app support feature. Simply navigate to the "Help" section within the user app, and you can find answers to frequently asked questions (FAQs) and submit support tickets.

b. WhatsApp Support: For more personalized assistance, you can reach out to our customer support team via WhatsApp. Save our official WhatsApp number +918800364332 to your contacts and initiate a conversation for prompt support.

c. Phone Support: If you prefer speaking to a customer support representative, you can call our dedicated support hotline at +918800364332. Our team will be happy to assist you during the specified hours.

2. Support Availability:

Our customer support team is available during the following hours:

Monday to Sunday: 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM (Local Time)

Please note that our support team will respond to inquiries received during these hours promptly. For inquiries received outside these hours, we will address them at the earliest during the next available support window.

3. Types of Support:

Our customer support team is well-equipped to handle various types of inquiries, including but not limited to:

  • General app usage assistance
  • Technical issues and troubleshooting
  • Payment-related queries
  • Fare estimation and billing inquiries
  • Driver-related concerns (e.g., behavior, navigation, etc.)
  • Feedback and suggestions

4. Response Time:

We strive to provide timely responses to all customer inquiries. During our operating hours, we aim to respond to WhatsApp messages and in-app support tickets within 24 hours and phone calls as promptly as possible. However, please allow for slight variations in response times based on the volume of inquiries.

5. Escalation Procedure:

If you feel that your inquiry or concern requires further attention, and you are dissatisfied with the initial resolution provided by our customer support team, you can request to escalate the matter to a supervisor or manager. Our escalation procedure will ensure that your concerns are addressed with utmost priority.

6. Feedback and Improvement:

At iDestination, we value your feedback. Whether it's a positive experience or an area where we can improve, we encourage you to share your thoughts with us. Your feedback will help us enhance our services and better cater to your needs.

We are committed to providing the best possible customer support experience. By following this policy, we aim to ensure that you have a smooth and enjoyable experience while using iDestination app.

Thank you for choosing iDestination. We look forward to serving you.
